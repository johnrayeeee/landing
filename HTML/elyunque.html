El Yunque National Forest, Puerto Rico

El Yunque National Forest is a tropical rainforest in northeastern Puerto Rico.
From El Portal Visitor Center, a walkway winds through the surrounding treetops. Trails
run to La Mina waterfall and Mount Britton, then up to the high-altitude dwarf forest.
In the south are ancient petroglyphs, made by the area’s indigenous Taíno people. The
forest comes alive with the nightly calls of coquí tree frogs.
